Yahya Ayyash

A hero of the resistance, a renowned martyr, a fearless engineer

Here's a time line of Yahya Ayyash's life:

  • 1966 - Born in Rafat, Palestine
  • 1985 - Graduated from high school
  • 1987 - Attends Birzeit University
  • 1991 - Finishes university and receive a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering
  • 1992 - Become active in the rank of Izz ad-Din Al-Qassam Brigade
  • 1993 - Israel military began their official hunt for him
  • 1994 - Yitzhak Rabin, former Prime Minister of Israel placed him as top target of his government's political and security priorities since he killed 71 Zionist occupiers using his handmade bombs
  • 1996 - dies at the age of 29
  • 2021 - Al-Qassam brigade launched the longest range missile in their arsenal, Ayyash 250 on Ramon International Airport in light of the Israeli aggression on the Gaza Strip in the battle of Saif Al-Quds
